About Us

In 1998 AMA joined hands with MAN Truck and Bus AG to create the initial foundation of the AMA Motors division that stands as an integral part of the AMA Group providing Transport solutions for a multitude of companies and industries on the Island. Since then AMA Motors has established partnerships with leading global manufacturers like New Holland Earth moving, ZF Driveline Technology, Liebherr Earth moving, Atlas, Kobelco Cranes, HELI,BOMAG and TowerLight. This select group of industry leaders helps us achieve our Core business idea of providing “Complete Transportation Solutions” for companies engaged in a variety of fields.
